About Me

My name is Jeffrey Arnell. My wife and I have been married for almost two years now and we currently reside in Provo, Utah with our two dogs (Lila and Iris).

I have been passionate about Computer Science ever since I first ran the "Hello World" project in Java while enrolled in AP Computer Science as a high school junior. I would often spend my free time using sites such as "Codecademy" to level up my skills.

I recently received a Bachelor's Degree in Software Development from Utah Valley University. I chose this university and degree due to the high level of practical learning and flexibility that allows me to work full time.

I enjoy working on personal software projects, spending time with friends and family, watching comedy television shows like "Community" and "Modern Family", and taking our two dogs on hikes.
